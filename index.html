A few words about my backstory

Five years in Paris were enough to realise I was in the wrong place in the wrong industry, so I packed my bags, gave up my career in the sound business and flew to the Yukon Territory for a one year working visa.

I met my awesome wife when I came back, fast forward a few years and we are now raising 2 kids!

While trying to figure out a new career path I started to sell my sound designer skills in the form of online products on third party sites. This went very well and got me started in online marketing, as I began to teach myself seo, blogging and coding to sell more of my sounds. Meanwhile, I went back to school and graduated a bachelor degree in business management.

Soon after graduating I started working for Qualifirst in Ray Martin's team, where I was given the chance to grow my dev skills and gradually become their ecommerce manager. I currently dedicate my time between Qualifirst and my other clients and own projects.

I purposefully live in a remote countryside in the south west of France and work from my home office. This is the best way to provide a healthy, happy, and secure lifestyle for my family. I intend to keep working remotely as long as possible as it suits me perfectly and allows me to be very productive.
